## 💡 Study Approach

**Pattern-Based Learning:** Problems are grouped by technique patterns, not just topic names. This helps you recognize when to apply specific algorithms during interviews.

**Key Patterns:**
- **Two Sum Pattern:** value → index HashMap
- **Prefix Sum + Hash:** prefix → frequency/index
- **Sliding Window:** dynamic window optimization
- **Monotonic Stack:** maintain increasing/decreasing order
- **Binary Search:** search space optimization
